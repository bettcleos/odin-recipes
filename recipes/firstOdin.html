ugali kienyeji recipe

ugali kienyeji is kenyan popular food which alot of people like due to ease in making and even the nutritional and health benefits which comes with it

Ingredients

  • spoonful of cooking oil
  • millet flour
  • 10g of salt
  • isagek "the vegetable"
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  1. boil isgek for 30mins
  2. drain it till it has no more water
  3. put cooking oil in the sufuria
  4. add chopped onions and cook it till a golden brown colour is achieved
  5. add tomatoes and allow it to cook for five minutes
  6. add the drained isagek and allow it cook for 10 minutes
  7. serve it with ugali
