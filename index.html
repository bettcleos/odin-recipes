ugali kienyeji recipe

ugali kienyeji is kenyan popular food which alot of people like due to ease in making and even the nutritional and health benefits which comes with it

Ingredients

  • spoonful of cooking oil
  • millet flour
  • 10g of salt
  • isagek "the vegetable"
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
